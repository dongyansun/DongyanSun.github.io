Hi, I’m Dongyan (Jack) Sun. I’m a BS/MS student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Southern California, where I work with Prof. Priya Panda and Prof. Viktor K. Prasanna.

My work develops structure-aware machine learning methods for Scientific ML and computer systems. My research interests include AI for science, machine learning for the physical sciences, intelligent computing systems, and machine learning methods that combine domain knowledge with modern AI.
